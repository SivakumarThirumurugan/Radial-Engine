### 🚀 Project Overview
This repository details the complete 3D mechanical design and assembly of a multi-cylinder radial engine. The objective of this project was to demonstrate advanced CAD proficiency in handling complex, multi-part assemblies with tight tolerances, intricate surface modeling, and dynamic kinematic relationships.

### 📈 Core Engineering Achievements

*   **Complex Assembly Architecture:** Engineered a highly detailed master rod and articulated connecting rod assembly, ensuring precise alignment and zero-interference motion within the crankcase.
*   **Kinematic Motion Validation:** Simulated the dynamic rotary motion of the crankshaft and piston timing to validate the mechanical feasibility of the stroke cycles.
*   **High-Fidelity Visual Rendering:** Applied advanced material textures, environmental lighting, and camera positioning to generate photorealistic renders that highlight the structural integrity and surface finish of the mechanical components.
*   **Parametric Control:** Utilized parametric modeling techniques to allow for rapid iterations of cylinder bore, stroke length, and overall engine displacement.

### ⚙️ Component Breakdown (Sub-Assemblies)
*   **Master & Articulating Rods:** High-stress linkage design for power transmission.
*   **Piston & Cylinder Heads:** Optimized for thermal dissipation (cooling fins) and compression.
*   **Crankshaft & Cam Ring:** Precision timing and rotary conversion mechanisms.
